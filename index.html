Leadership is the capacity to translate vision into reality.

—Warren Bennis

It is such leaders' contributions that result in the advancement of any profession, and recognizing the efforts of such promising leaders is an ethical obligation of any professional. Hence, on the occasion of the approaching superannuation of one of the most influential library professionals, a devoted educator, and a decisive leader in the library science field, Dr. Mohan R. Kherde, Director, Knowledge Resource Centre, Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University a Festschrift is scheduled to be released shortly.

It is for this reason that the journey of a librarian and the library profession is going to be celebrated through this Festschrift. "Seventy-Five Years of Indian Library Profession," the Festscrift theme, provides an opportunity to reflect on what the last 75 years have brought to the library profession. This festschrift is a reflection of the professional journey and outstanding contributions of Dr. Mohan Kherde, who has inspired all the library professionals in the country to set directions for their lives and work.

About Dr. Mohan R. Kherde

Dr. Mohan R. Kherde has been working as a Director of the Knowledge Resource Centre of Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University (SGBAU), Amravati since 2008. He started his career as Librarian at G. S. College, Khamgaon where he worked from 1989 to 1991. Then he joined Matorshri Vimalabai Deshmukh Mahavidhyalaya, Amravati where he worked from1991 to 2008. Dr. Mohan Kherde has had a distinguished career as a Librarian of renowned academic Libraries. He would teach in the department of Library & Information Science of SGBAU. He was the Ex-Course Co-ordinator for the LIS course of Yashvantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University, Nashik. He showed demonstrable experience in handling quality issues, assessment and accreditation procedures, etc. He was the sole agent to bring the desired transformation from a traditional outlook to the modernized one of the KRC of SGBAU.

Some of his notable achievements concerning the development of KRC are mentioned below:

  • Developed the KRC portal (www.sgbaukrc.ac.in) by making quality resources available.
  • Developed Enabling Unit for differently-abled persons.
  • Implemented successfully all the major schemes of INFLIBNET.
  • Initiated the work of Institutional Repository.
  • Extended the facility of remote access and federated search.
  • Developed E-Resources Access Center for SGBAU-Knowledge Resource Centre.
  • Developed the Open Consortia for college titled “Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University Library Consortia for UG and PG Colleges" for subscribing to e-resources.
  • Developed Learning Management System for the University.
  • Provided Study Centre facility to the students at various colleges affiliated with the University through a Memorandum of Agreement.
  • Approval of Rs. 5 Crore for the Residential Knowledge Resource Centre from the State Government. The work is under process.
  • Development of Air cooled and well-furnished Periodical Section from the Grant received (Rs. 40 Lacs) from the UGC.
  • Took the initiative to upgrade the professional skills of the librarians working in SGBAU-affiliated colleges.

He completed one minor research project on “Bibliographical Control of Library and Information Science Literature in Vidarbha Region” funded by UGC during 2001-2003. In another project of UGC under the Shodhganga scheme, he digitized the theses awarded by Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University, Amravati. He equally was efficient to establish the Centre for Persons with Disabilities at the expense of Rs. 8,00,000 under UGC’s Merged Scheme for Persons with Disabilities.

He wrote two books and edited one. He also edited two conference proceedings. He published 25 research papers wherein 9 were published in international journals. He published 8 book chapters and published 24 conference papers.

He is the chief editor of "The Journal of Indian Library Association" and can be seen on the editorial board of the "International Journal of Research in Library Science", "International Journal of Information Resources and Knowledge Management", "Dhnyan Gangotri", Research Port, and KLA Bulletin. He delivered more than 70 speeches at several conferences, seminars, and workshops. Seven students have completed their Ph. Ds under his supervision while 5 are in the process of submission. He even guided 4 M.Phil. students and 43 PG students in their dissertation work. He is a renowned professional visiting as a resource person for eminent conferences, seminars, and workshops. His tremendous administrative skill and desire to educate library professionals can be seen in the organization of more than 20 different types of conferences and seminars. He has also worked in different capacities at many eminent positions in Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University, Amravati; Yashwantrao Chavan Maharashtra Open University, Nashik; Rashtrasant Tukdoji Maharaj Nagpur University, Nagpur; North Maharashtra University, Jalgaon and Swami Ramanand Marathwada University, Nanded. He is the recipient of the ILA-Gidwani-Deshpande Best Academic Librarian Award given by ILA, Delhi in 2014. He was an excellent sports player during his college life. Before accepting librarianship as full-time profession, he worked as a professional artist in many theatrical performances. He is also fond of music.

Theme

We invite the LIS professionals to take part in this endeavor of honoring a library professional, who has made significant contributions to the cause of the LIS profession and promoted valuable services in the University library system. In recognition of his contributions and everlasting interest in Library Management and Librarianship, the theme of the festschrift volume has been chosen as “Seventy-Five Years of Indian Library Profession” with the following sub-themes:

Subthemes
  • Dr. Mohan Kherde as a Person, Friend, Teacher, Professional, and Guide
  • Plagiarism and Academic Libraries; Academic Integrity & Credibility: Research Quality, Ethical Issues, Policies
  • Expected Librarianship in the Future; Current Issue in Librarianship; Skills in Librarianship; Embedded Librarianship; Professional Skills in Librarianship; Information Literacy Movement
  • Role of Libraries in Higher Education and Research; Reengineering of Libraries; Libraries and NEP; Library Services
  • Social Media and Technology; Application in Libraries; Social Networking Websites, Tools and Technology, and SNSs.
  • Digital Libraries; Digital Archiving; Digital Preservation; Artificial Intelligence; Machine Learning; Data Mining for Digital Libraries; IPR and Professional Ethics in Digital Environment.
  • Collection Development: The Approach of Academic Libraries; CDP in Digital Environment E-resources; E-books: Current Status and Future; Case studies
  • Public Libraries and School Libraries: Current Status and Expected Role; Partnership between Public and Academic Libraries
  • Research in Librarianship; Research Innovation and Ranking: Practice and Implication; Research Information Management; Data Repositories; Data Sharing; Data Visualization; Big Data.

The Festschrift in honor of Dr. Mohan R. Kherde will be published during the felicitation in honor of him. The event will be organized in the first week of June.

Submission Guidelines

All the authors, researchers, library professionals are invited to send the quality book chapters/research paper following MS Word format only typed in 12 font size (Times New Roman), single space, A4 size format following APA style of reference management.

Each submission should contain the specific title followed by name (in full), designation and affiliation of the contributor(s). The submission should also carry contact address, telephone, fax number and email address. An informative abstract of about 100-150 words should be included. Acronyms and abbreviations should be spelt out in full when first used in text. The citations should be numbered as superscripts in the text in numerical order and should be listed in the same order at the end of the text. Following pattern should be followed for reference to be used.
